Why no date or region on operational programs
Operational programs (scoring, routing, webhooks, data quality) run continuously and don't "launch" the way a campaign does. Adobe's own guidance sets these up as a single program named for the function, with Channel = Operational — separate from the campaign hierarchy entirely, so they're never mistaken for something to archive by date.
Number in increments of 10 (010, 020, 030…) so you can insert a new step later (e.g. 015) without renaming everything downstream.
